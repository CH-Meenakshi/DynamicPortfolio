About PortfolioGen

Empowering professionals to showcase their skills and achievements

Our Story

PortfolioGen was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to help professionals create stunning, job-winning portfolios with ease. We recognized that many talented individuals struggle to present their skills effectively, often spending hours designing portfolios that still don't meet professional standards.

Our team of designers and developers came together to create a solution - a platform that combines beautiful, recruiter-approved templates with an intuitive editor that anyone can use.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves to have a professional portfolio that showcases their unique talents and achievements. Our mission is to democratize access to high-quality portfolio design, making it easy and affordable for students, chefs, writers, and professionals from all fields to create portfolios that get them noticed.
